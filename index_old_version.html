About This Project

This project aims to support children with autism spectrum disorder through an innovative virtual reality application focused on physical education and motor skills development. Our VR application provides an engaging, safe, and structured environment where children can practice and develop essential physical and social skills at their own pace.

The application has been developed as part of an eTwinning project, bringing together educators, researchers, and specialists to create evidence-based tools that enhance learning outcomes for children with autism. The VR experience is carefully designed to promote physical activity, coordination, social interaction, and confidence-building in a supportive virtual environment.

Installation Guide

1

Download SideQuest: Install the SideQuest app on your computer from sidequestvr.com. This tool allows you to sideload applications to your Meta Quest headset.

2

Enable Developer Mode: In your Meta Quest app, go to Settings > Developer Mode and enable it. You may need to create a developer account with Meta.

3

Connect Your Headset: Connect your Meta Quest headset to your computer using a USB-C cable. Open SideQuest and allow the connection when prompted on your headset.

4

Install the APK: In SideQuest, click the 'Install APK file from folder' button and select the downloaded APK file. Wait for the installation to complete.

5

Launch the Application: On your Meta Quest, go to App Library > Filter (Unknown Sources) to find and launch the installed VR application.
